Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

Overview

Genius Cleaner is committed to protecting your privacy. This extension is designed with privacy as a core principle - we do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

Data Collection

We do not collect any data. Specifically:

  • No personal information is collected
  • No browsing history is recorded
  • No usage analytics or telemetry
  • No cookies or tracking mechanisms
  • No data is sent to external servers

Local Storage

The extension stores your preferences locally using Chrome's built-in storage API:

  • Settings: Your toggle preferences (what to hide/show) are stored locally in your browser
  • Sync: If you're signed into Chrome, your settings may sync across your devices through Chrome's built-in sync feature

This data never leaves your browser except through Chrome's own sync mechanism if enabled.

Permissions

The extension requires the following permissions:

  • genius.com: To modify the Genius website's appearance by hiding elements
  • storage: To save your preferences locally
  • activeTab: To apply settings to the current Genius tab

Third Parties

Genius Cleaner does not share data with any third parties because we do not collect any data in the first place. The extension operates entirely within your browser.

Open Source

Genius Cleaner is open source. You can review the complete source code on GitHub to verify our privacy practices.

Changes

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.
